Faith, hope, and love in action every day

pastors message

Your generosity makes a profound impact

George Macharia is the founder and lead pastor of City Lighters Church. He has a deeply-rooted belief in the local church and a relentless passion to inspire a generation of believers to fulfil their God-given destiny. Through his leadership City Lighters has grown from a group of 7 people in a small room to a church of hundreds of people. George Macharia is a visionary leader with a passion for the cause of Christ and the building of His Church. Known for his dynamic preaching gift, George delivers messages that resonate in hearts and challenge mindsets. He lives to encourage people to boldly step into the plan that God has for them..
